Dissertation & Thesis Procedures

Step-by-step procedures and downloadable forms. All forms available here — no need to request individually.

📘 Doctoral Degree (PhD) 📗 Master's Degree
⚠️ Students must complete and obtain approval for each step before proceeding. All G.S. forms must be approved by advisors and the Dean prior to advancement.

Doctoral program — 32-step procedure · Announced April 2026

Phase 1 — Coursework & Setup
1

Coursework

Complete all required coursework

Students must complete all required coursework before advancing to the dissertation phase.

2

Examination

Qualifying Examination (QE)

Students must pass the Qualifying Examination before proceeding to dissertation title development.

3

Advisor

Dissertation title development with advisor

Work with your assigned advisor to develop your dissertation title and research direction. Submit the concept paper.

G.S.3 Advisor Appointment G.S.4 Dissertation Concept Paper
Phase 2 — Dissertation Title
4

Title Submission

Submit dissertation title (G.S.5 + G.S.5-1)

Submit your dissertation title with supportive information to the Graduate School for committee consideration.

G.S.5 Title Approval Form G.S.5-1 Supportive Information
5–6

Approval

Committee approval & Graduate School announcement

Obtain committee approval for your title. The Graduate School will officially announce approved titles. Use G.S.5-2 if title changes later.

G.S.5-2 Title Change Request
Phase 3 — 3-Chapter Proposal
7

Writing

Develop 3-chapter proposal

Write your 3-chapter proposal. Consistently consult your advisor and incorporate all feedback to ensure the research meets required standards.

G.S.7 Guide Proposal Writing Guidelines
8

Request

Submit 3-chapter examination request

Upon advisor approval, submit G.S.7 with 5 copies of your 3-chapter proposal to the Graduate School for scheduling.

⏱ Submit at least 30 days before the examination. Doctoral degree: 5 copies required.

G.S.7 Proposal Examination Request
9–11

Examination

3-Chapter examination → Revisions → Results announcement

Take the 3-chapter examination. Make revisions based on committee feedback. Graduate School announces students who have passed.

Phase 4 — Research Process
12

Instrument

Develop research instrument

Develop the research instrument tailored to your research to obtain IOC (Index of Item-Objective Congruence).

IOC IOC Assessment Template
13

Expert Evaluation

Invite 5 experts to evaluate the instrument

Submit G.S.8 to invite 5 qualified experts. Graduate School will issue official invitation letters. IOC determines whether the instrument can proceed.

G.S.8 Invitation to Specialists
14

Pilot Study

Instrument testing — pilot study (n = 30)

After IOC approval, submit G.S.9 with IOC results to conduct a pilot study with 30 participants. Data analysis will determine Cronbach's Alpha.

G.S.9 Instrument Testing Letter
15

Data Collection

Collect data from target population

After instrument approval, submit G.S.10 to collect data from the full target population and analyze research results.

G.S.10 Data Collection Letter
16

Writing

Prepare 5-chapter dissertation

Write the complete 5-chapter dissertation summarizing all research findings.

📌 Begin working on your publication article during this stage.

Phase 5 — Publication
17

Publication

Obtain approval to publish & receive acceptance letters

Submit G.S.11-1 to advisor then Graduate School. Two articles required for doctoral completion. Attach acceptance letters to G.S.11 for the defense request.

⚠️ Beware of predatory/cloned journals. All articles must be based on your dissertation research.

G.S.11-1 Publication Approval Form
18–19

Dissertation

Submit 5-chapter dissertation to advisor & revise

Submit the full dissertation to advisors for review. Revise based on all feedback and suggestions before requesting the oral examination.

Phase 6 — Oral Examination (Defense)
20

Compliance

Plagiarism check (1st) & AI usage declaration

Conduct plagiarism detection — similarity must not exceed 30%. Submit G.S.14 with the report. Complete AI usage declaration. Both required for the examination request (Step 21).

G.S.14 Plagiarism Detection Report G.S.AI AI Usage Declaration
21

Request

Submit oral examination request

Submit G.S.11 with all required documents: G.S.11-1, publication acceptance letters, G.S.14, G.S.AI, and 5 copies of dissertation.

⏱ Submit at least 30 days before examination. Doctoral fee: 8,000 THB.

G.S.11 Examination Request G.S.11-1 Publication Approval G.S.14 Plagiarism Report G.S.AI AI Declaration
22–23

Defense

Dissertation defense & post-defense revisions

Present and defend your dissertation. Make all required revisions within the deadline set by the committee (15, 30, or 45 days).

Phase 7 — Format Check & Graduation
24–25

Format Check

Format review & revision

Submit G.S.12 with 1 copy of the completed dissertation for format and abstract review. Revise as directed.

G.S.12 Format Review Request
26

Compliance

Plagiarism check (2nd) & AI declaration

Second plagiarism check and AI declaration required after all revisions. Both must be attached to the graduation application (Step 27).

G.S.14 Plagiarism Report G.S.AI AI Declaration
27

Graduation

Apply for graduation — submit 5 hardbound copies

Submit 5 hardbound copies of the dissertation together with all required forms to the Graduate School.

G.S.12 Format Review G.S.13 Graduation Request G.S.14 Plagiarism Report G.S.AI AI Declaration G.S.15 Publication Cert. G.S.16 Dissertation Submission
28–30

Approval Chain

Graduate School → Academic Council → University Council

Graduation application is reviewed through three approval levels before degrees are officially conferred.

31–32

Completion

Transcript issuance & collection

Official transcripts are issued and ready for collection. Processing takes approximately 7–15 working days after all approvals are complete.

Master's degree — thesis & independent study procedures

1

Advisor

Advisor appointment

Identify your major advisor and co-advisor (if applicable). Submit the appointment form for committee approval.

G.S.3 Advisor Appointment
2

Title

Submit title & obtain approval

Thesis: submit G.S.5 + G.S.5-1. Independent Study: submit G.S.6. Obtain advisor and Graduate School approval.

G.S.5 Thesis Title G.S.5-1 Supportive Info G.S.5-2 Title Change G.S.6 Indep. Study Title
3

Proposal

Develop & submit proposal for examination

Write the 3-chapter proposal. After advisor approval, submit G.S.7 with 3 copies for the proposal examination.

⏱ Submit at least 30 days before examination. Master's degree: 3 copies required.

G.S.7 Proposal Exam Request G.S.7 Guide Writing Guidelines
4

Research

Instrument development, expert review & data collection

Develop instrument → invite experts for IOC (G.S.8) → pilot test with 30 participants (G.S.9) → collect data (G.S.10).

G.S.8 Invite Specialists G.S.9 Instrument Testing G.S.10 Data Collection
5

Defense Request

Submit oral examination request

Submit G.S.11 with plagiarism report (G.S.14), AI declaration, publication approval (G.S.11-1), and 3 copies of thesis.

⏱ Submit at least 30 days before examination. Fee: 2,000–4,000 THB depending on program.

G.S.11 Examination Request G.S.11-1 Publication Approval G.S.14 Plagiarism Report G.S.AI AI Declaration
6

Defense

Oral examination & post-defense revisions

Defend your thesis before the committee. Make all required revisions within the assigned deadline (15, 30, or 45 days).

7

Graduation

Format check, plagiarism (2nd) & graduation application

Submit G.S.12 for format review. Apply for graduation with all required forms and 5 hardbound copies.

G.S.12 Format Review G.S.13 Graduation Request G.S.14 Plagiarism Report G.S.AI AI Declaration G.S.15 Publication Cert. G.S.16 Thesis Submission
